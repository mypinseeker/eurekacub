# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open, welcoming, and **child-safe** environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

**Child safety is our #1 priority.** EurekaCub is built for children. Every decision we make — from code to content to community interactions — must put child welfare first.

## Our Standards

### Child Safety Standards

These are non-negotiable and take precedence over all other considerations:

1. **No collection of personal data from children.** The application must never ask for, store, or transmit personally identifiable information from children (names, ages, locations, photos, etc.).

2. **No external links or redirects.** All content must be self-contained. No links to external websites, social media, or third-party services in any user-facing content.

3. **No chat or messaging features.** Children must not be able to communicate with other users or strangers through the application.

4. **No advertising or commercial content.** No ads, product placements, or links to commercial services.

5. **No content that could frighten, shame, or pressure children.** This includes:
   - No timers or time pressure
   - No rankings, leaderboards, or competitive comparisons
   - No punishment mechanics (loss of points, negative sounds, "wrong" labels)
   - No scary imagery or themes
   - No content that could make a child feel inadequate

6. **Age-appropriate content only.** All puzzles, stories, illustrations, and text must be appropriate for the labeled age group (6+ or 9+).

7. **Report immediately.** If you discover any content or behavior that could harm children, report it immediately to the maintainers. Do not wait for the next review cycle.

### Community Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and especially for the children who use EurekaCub
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Reviewing contributions with care, especially content that children will interact with

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Submitting content that violates child safety standards (see above)
- Deliberately introducing content designed to bypass safety reviews
- Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting or harmful to children

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, or to ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for other behaviors that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

**For child safety violations:** Maintainers will act immediately. Content that violates child safety standards will be removed without waiting for discussion. Contributors who deliberately introduce harmful content will be permanently banned.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

- GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, discussions, code)
- Project communication channels
- Project events and meetups
- Any space where an individual is representing the project or its community

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

**Child safety reports** will be treated with the highest priority and investigated immediately.

The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident. Further details of specific enforcement policies may be posted separately.

Project maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by other members of the project's leadership.

## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Correction

**Community Impact:** Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcome.

**Consequence:** A private, written warning, providing clarity around the nature of the violation and an explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning

**Community Impact:** A violation through a single incident or series of actions.

**Consequence:** A warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with the people involved for a specified period. This includes avoiding interactions in community spaces as well as external channels. Violating these terms may lead to a temporary or permanent ban.

### 3. Temporary Ban

**Community Impact:** A serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.

**Consequence:** A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication with the community for a specified period. No public or private interaction with the people involved is allowed during this period. Violating these terms may lead to a permanent ban.

### 4. Permanent Ban

**Community Impact:** Demonstrating a pattern of violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior, harassment, or **any deliberate violation of child safety standards**.

**Consequence:** A permanent ban from any sort of public interaction within the community.
